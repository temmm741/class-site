TRASH
CONFERENCE

Join the first TRASH CONFERENCE to take place in the Pacific Ocean. Since people from all over the world have been ignoring the necessity of recycling and producing less waste, we have formed TRASHCON. Here consumers and producers will compete to collect the most waste from the Great Pacific garbage patch. In order to qualify participants must remove a minimum of 200 pounds of waste and recyclables from the Patch.

The competitor to collect the most waste will receive a $1 million dollar ca$h prize to kick start the production of their latest eco-friendly gadget. Please bring product design blueprints to prove that your product is at minimum, 85% recyclable. The conference will take place rain or shine. Better bring your big nets people!
